# #NFC: Nexus S teardown: How NFC fits inside the new Google phone

According to Google: "Nexus S can read information from 'smart' tags, or everyday objects that have NFC chips in them. These can be anything from stickers and movie posters to t-shirts."
The handset's NFC antenna can be found glued to the inside of the battery cover. It is pressure connected by two gold contacts on the main body of the handset.
The NXP NFC controller and associated components can be found on a daughterboard that also holds the SIM cradle, close to the contacts that connect the antenna.
